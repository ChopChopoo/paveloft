Paveloft

Long miles, soft landings.

Paveloft was started in 2022 in Asheville, North Carolina, with one goal: build a max-cushion shoe that does not feel disconnected from the road. The CloudWeave midsole is a layered construction that places a firmer carrier foam under a softer top layer, so the runner gets the cushioning of a high-stack shoe without the wandering feeling that often comes with one. The brand sells directly online and through specialty running stores in the Southeast. The line is two long-run shoes and one walking-and-recovery model.
